The Architect

Sarvesh

Chartered Accountant / Trading Mentor / Inner Alignment

Sarvesh Mopkar is a Chartered Accountant, experienced trend-following trader, and mentor with more than 15 years of experience in the financial markets. His work brings together structure, long-range thinking, and a deeply grounded understanding of human behaviour.

Beyond trading, he helps people develop psychological mastery over money by cultivating consistency, discipline, emotional intelligence, and an abundance mindset. He also explores the connection between inner alignment and outer results through sound healing, energy work, astrology, and self-awareness.

Through his mentorship and writing, Sarvesh empowers people to move beyond fear and impulsive behaviour, and to discover the invisible edge that comes from patience, clarity, and disciplined execution.

Partner / Co-owner

Harshaa

Emotional Healing / Embodied Awareness / Tarot

Harshaa's path has been centred more directly on emotional healing and embodied awareness. Her work supports people in reconnecting with their feelings, their bodies, and their natural liveliness with gentleness and care.

Through practices such as movement, energy work, and Tarot, she creates spaces where emotional blocks can soften, inner truth can surface, and a deeper sense of ease can be rediscovered from within.
