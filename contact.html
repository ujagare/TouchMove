How do I book a session?

You can book directly through our Contact page. Share your intention and preferred timing, and our team will confirm the best available slot.

Are sessions available online?

Yes. We offer online sessions worldwide, and in-person sessions in Pune, India.

What should I prepare before a session?

Come with a calm space, an open mind, and the key questions or areas where you want clarity. No special ritual is required.

Can I choose between tarot and movement guidance?

Absolutely. You can choose one approach or a blended session based on your current goals and comfort.
