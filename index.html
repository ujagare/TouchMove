What “Touch and Move” Means

The name reflects two fundamental aspects of human healing and transformation.

energy_savings_leaf

Touch

Touch represents presence, connection, and healing. Through mindful and respectful body-based practices, individuals can release tension, reconnect with their bodies, and restore a sense of safety and relaxation. Touch in this context is intentional, therapeutic, and consent-based, creating a space where the body can naturally relax and restore balance.

air

Move

Movement represents life, flow, and emotional expression. Through practices such as dance movement therapy, breathwork, and embodied awareness, individuals overcome their emotional patterns and reconnect with their natural vitality. Movement allows the body to express and integrate experiences that are often difficult to process through thinking alone.
